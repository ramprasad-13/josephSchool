Our Founding Story at St. Joseph's

"Saint Joseph's English Medium High School was established as the first English Medium High School in the year 1993 by V. Joy Joseph in Singarayakonda. Later, in 2002, the Ulavapadu branch was started by Varghese. Our school has now completed nearly three decades in the realm of education, during which the institution has fostered numerous achievers and inspirers who continue to make their mark across the globe today."

Our Key Differentiators

Competitive Edge

Achieve Best Performance in State and National Level competitive exams and gain an edge with IIT Foundation Classes.

Skills & Development

Focus on communication skill development, plus co-curricular activities like yoga, dance, music, karate, and frequent Cultural Programmes.

Modern Infrastructure

Utilize Digital Classrooms, state-of-the-art Computer Lab, Science Lab, and extensive facilities for Games & Sports activities.

Engagement & Motivation

Boost confidence through Seminars, Quiz Programmes, Debates, and frequent Motivation Classes for personal growth.

Personalized Support

Dedicated Special Care for Slow Learners, coupled with Daily Test Papers and a focused Morning & Evening Study Hours schedule.

Diverse Faculty

Benefit from our Well-qualified staff recruited from across the region, including Kerala, Tamilnadu, Andhra Pradesh, and Odisha.
